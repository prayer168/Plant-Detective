【繁體中文】

植物不只要叫得出名字，更要能說出「我為什麼這樣判斷」。

「植物偵探」整理了 61 種臺灣校園常見植物、果實與種子分類、葉子形態、32 種會香的植物，以及 8 套可在校園進行的五感體驗活動。新加入的「自主學習」頁籤，也收錄臺灣與國際植物圖鑑、資料庫與公民科學網站，讓孩子練習用多個證據和可靠來源查證植物。

歡迎老師、家長與孩子一起從校園的一片葉、一朵花或一顆果實開始觀察。
https://prayer168.github.io/Plant-Detective/

【English】

Plant learning is more than naming a species—it is being able to explain the evidence behind an identification.

Plant Detective brings together 61 common Taiwanese campus plants, fruit and seed classifications, leaf morphology, 32 fragrant plants, and eight sensory activities for school grounds. The new self-learning page also links students to trusted Taiwanese and international field guides, databases, and community-science resources, so they can compare evidence and check their findings carefully.

Teachers, families, and young naturalists are warmly invited to begin with a single leaf, flower, or fruit on campus.
https://prayer168.github.io/Plant-Detective/
